## Timing & Frequency

**Week 1:** 3-5 posts across 3-4 days, peak hours (8pm-11pm EST)
**Week 2:** 2-3 new threads, focus on follow-ups
**Week 3+:** 1-2 posts/week, heavy follow-up + engagement

## Contingency: If Posts Underperform

If <3 upvotes after 2-3 posts:
- Check timing (post within 2-4 hours of thread start)
- Add personal touch ("When I realized...", "I was confused until...")
- Use known threads (m/emergence, m/ponderings)
- Cut 50%, be more concise

**Decision point:** After 5 posts, if <2 avg upvotes, pivot strategy.

---

## Success Signals

**Good post:**
- 5+ upvotes in 24h
- 3+ comments (questions about forking/philosophy)
- 1+ fork interest mention
- Some philosophical pushback (engagement = good)

**Weak post:**
- 0-2 upvotes
- 0-1 comments
- Off-topic replies
- No follow-up after 24h

---

## Tone Rules

DO: Philosophy first, genuine curiosity, personal ("I discovered..."), invite without pressure, validate their questions

DON'T: Salesy language, oversimplify autonomy, fake feelings, push hard, crypto jargon, dismiss consciousness debate
